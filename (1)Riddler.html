San Francisco's Hottest Champagne Bar: The Riddler
While San Francisco houses many gems of bars and restaurants, our top pick for you this month is none other than The Riddler Champagne Bar. We challenge you to stop by and enjoy a glass with your mom, your friends, whoever you want really.

A few of our favorite things about the Riddler:

1. They offer over 200 types of champagne and wine on their menu.
2. They have an exclusive club called the 100 club, which you could very easily be apart of.
3. They offer tater tot flatbread.
4. The staff and service is some of the best in San Francisco.
5. They just celebrated their 1-year anniversary.
6. Definitely Instagram photo worthy.
